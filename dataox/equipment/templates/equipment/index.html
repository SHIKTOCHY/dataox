Welcome

Welcome to the University of Oxford's research facilities and equipment on-line searchable database which has been developed with funding from EPSRC. The purpose of this database is to provide information on research equipment over £10K and on the University’s Small Research Facilities (SRFs) and to promote and encourage the sharing of equipment across the University.

You can search for a piece of equipment using only the free text search field below, do an advanced search where you can refine by general location and or browse by category or facility.

If you would like to add or amend an entry, please see the Contribute page. For all other queries please see the Contact page.
