Vacancy Treemap

Please click on an individual department to view the vacancies within it.

Details

This shows a breakdown (by department) of the vacancies currently available on the University of Oxford's recruitment website and/or www.jobs.ac.uk.

Left-click on a department to see the breakdown of vacancies within that department if it has any subdepartments. Right-click to move to its parent department.

The Treemap is powered by the Google Chart Tools.

Note: some RSS feeders (eg Outlook) convert URLs to lowercase before subscribing, yet URLs are case sensitive. In this situation, please use a service such as http://bit.ly to change one of our links to a lower-case URL.
