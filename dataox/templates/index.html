Home

Welcome to data.ox.ac.uk, the University of Oxford's institutional linked open data store.

Aims

The site aims to collect data from around the University and make it available for reuse as linked data under open licenses.

Exploring the data

To get started, have a look at our datasets or use the explore page to get a flavour of what's available.

All data are also available through our SPARQL endpoint. The site also supports content negotiation, allowing you to request data in a variety of RDF serializations.

Getting involved

Are you are a data owner within the University with data you'd like to publish? We can help with transforming your data into RDF and automating the publishing of your data on the data.ox.ac.uk infrastructure. For more information, feel free to e-mail the team.

If you've got an example of something produced using data from this site then we'd be delighted to hear from you so we can include it in our list of examples.
